SkyTeam ROBLOX proudly welcomes Korean Air as its newest member airline, marking a new era of collaboration and excellence within the SkyTeam Alliance. This milestone represents not only an expansion of SkyTeam’s virtual global network but also a renewed commitment to quality, innovation, and immersive experiences.

---

> Owned by **Featbuzz** and **SILXS**, Korean Air ROBLOX emerges as a reimagined flag
> carrier for Korea, representing a new beginning in leadership and identity
> following the previous iteration of Korean Air. With a refreshed brand and
> visual identity, the airline carries forward a vision to deliver the highest
> level of service, safety, and customer care to its passengers across our network.

![Lounge](/images/korean/interiorplane.png)

<Caption>
	By joining SkyTeam, Korean Air contributes to the alliance’s strategic focus
	on enhancing the passenger experience and expanding connectivity across the
	virtual world. Members and passengers will enjoy seamless connections to new

	growing roster of member airlines.
</Caption>
