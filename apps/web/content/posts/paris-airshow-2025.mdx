We are pleased to announce that SkyTeam ROBLOX will be exhibiting the International Paris Le Bourget Air Show with our 10 incredible allies bringing together the latest technological innovations in the community.

From the creators of the infamous Farnborough International Airshow, our partnership will spark a new era of roaviation, showcasing groundbreaking technologies and immersive experiences coming soon to the SkyTeam Alliance.

## SKYTEAM PARK

SkyTeam will be concepting our own central hub for our 10 allies within the air show called "SkyTeam Park". This immersive space will feature pavilions for each of our allies with phenomenal architecture designed by our lead developer.

Enjoy exclusive attractions like airline cultural experiences and connect with developers and industry experts within this space to get an insight into what's next.
